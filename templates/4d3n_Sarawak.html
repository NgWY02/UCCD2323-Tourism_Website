Kuching City & Cultural Exploration

Begin your Sarawak adventure in Kuching, the vibrant capital city. Explore its historical landmarks, cultural sites, and delicious food scene.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Kuching Waterfront: Stroll along the scenic waterfront, admire the Sarawak River, and enjoy the city's skyline.
    • Sarawak Museum: Discover the state's rich history, culture, and natural wonders through its exhibits.
    • Tua Pek Kong Temple: Visit one of the oldest Chinese temples in Kuching, known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful atmosphere.
    • Cat Museum: Learn about the feline fascination in Kuching and see a collection of cat-related artifacts.

  • Activities:
    • Explore the Main Bazaar: Shop for souvenirs, handicrafts, and local products.
    • Try Sarawak Laksa: Savor this iconic dish, a must-try for any visitor to Kuching.
    • Take a Sarawak River Cruise: Enjoy a scenic boat ride and admire the city from a different perspective.
2
Semenggoh Nature Reserve: Orangutan Encounter

Witness the gentle giants of Borneo at the Semenggoh Nature Reserve, a rehabilitation center for orangutans.

  • Highlights:
    • Orangutan Feeding: Observe these magnificent creatures during their feeding sessions.
    • Nature Trails: Explore the rainforest trails and discover the diverse flora and fauna.

  • Tips:
    • Check the feeding times in advance and arrive early to secure a good viewing spot.
    • Respect the orangutans and maintain a safe distance.
    • Bring a camera to capture these unforgettable moments.
3
Bako National Park: Rainforest & Wildlife

Embark on a day trip to Bako National Park, a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

  • Highlights:
    • Jungle Trekking: Hike through the rainforest trails, ranging from easy walks to challenging climbs.
    • Wildlife Spotting: Look out for proboscis monkeys, long-tailed macaques, bearded pigs, and other fascinating creatures.
    • Beach Relaxation: Unwind on the secluded beaches and enjoy the natural beauty.

  • Tips:
    • Wear comfortable hiking shoes and bring plenty of water.
    • Be prepared for the rainforest environment with insect repellent and sunscreen.
    • Check the tide timings if you plan to visit the beaches.
4
Sarawak Cultural Village: Living Museum

Conclude your Sarawak trip with a visit to the Sarawak Cultural Village, a living museum showcasing the diverse ethnic cultures of Sarawak.

  • Highlights:
    • Traditional Houses: Explore replicas of traditional houses from various ethnic groups.
    • Cultural Performances: Witness captivating dances, music, and rituals.
    • Hands-on Activities: Try your hand at traditional crafts like weaving and beadwork.

  • Tips:
    • Check the showtimes for cultural performances.
    • Allow ample time to explore the village and participate in activities.
    • Purchase souvenirs and handicrafts to support local artisans.

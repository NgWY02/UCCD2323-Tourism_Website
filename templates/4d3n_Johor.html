Johor Bahru City Exploration

Kickstart your Johor journey in the bustling city of Johor Bahru, filled with family-friendly attractions and cultural landmarks.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Legoland Malaysia: Immerse yourselves in a world of LEGO bricks and enjoy thrilling rides and attractions.
    • Angry Birds Activity Park: Have a fun-filled day with interactive games and activities based on the popular Angry Birds franchise.
    • Sultan Abu Bakar State Mosque: Admire the grandeur of this historic mosque, perched on a hill with stunning views.

  • Activities:
    • Explore Johor Bahru City Square: Shop for souvenirs, try local delicacies, and experience the city's vibrant atmosphere.
    • Visit the Arulmigu Sri Rajakaliamman Glass Temple: Marvel at the intricate glasswork adorning this unique Hindu temple.
2
Desaru Coast: Sun, Sand, and Sea

Head to the picturesque Desaru Coast, a premier beach destination with luxurious resorts, exciting waterparks, and pristine beaches.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Desaru Coast Adventure Waterpark: Experience thrilling water slides, wave pools, and lazy rivers.
    • Desaru Fruit Farm: Take a tour of this sprawling fruit farm and sample a variety of tropical fruits.
    • Firefly River Cruise: Embark on a magical night cruise to witness the enchanting display of fireflies along the riverbanks.

  • Activities:
    • Relax on the beach: Soak up the sun, swim in the clear waters, and enjoy the peaceful ambiance.
    • Play golf: Tee off at one of the championship golf courses in the area.
    • Visit the Desaru Ostrich Farm: Get up close with these fascinating birds and learn about their unique characteristics.
3
Cultural Immersion in Muar

Experience the rich cultural heritage and culinary delights of Muar, a historic town known for its traditional architecture and delicious food.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Sultan Ibrahim Jamek Mosque: Admire the beautiful architecture of this mosque, a prominent landmark in Muar.
    • Muar Street Art: Stroll through the streets and discover vibrant murals and street art.
    • **Tanjung Emas Recreational Park:** Enjoy a leisurely walk or picnic in this scenic park by the river.

  • Culinary Experiences:
    • Mee Bandung Muar: Try this iconic noodle dish, a specialty of Muar.
    • Otak-Otak: Sample this flavorful grilled fish cake, a popular local snack.
    • Asam Pedas: Indulge in this tangy and spicy fish stew, a must-try dish in Muar.
4
Endau Rompin National Park: Nature's Wonderland

Conclude your trip with an adventure in Endau Rompin National Park, one of the oldest rainforests in the world.

  • Highlights:
    • Jungle Trekking: Explore the park's diverse trails, ranging from easy walks to challenging hikes.
    • Waterfall Abseiling: Experience the thrill of rappelling down a cascading waterfall.
    • Wildlife Spotting: Keep an eye out for rare and endangered species like tigers, elephants, and tapirs.
    • River Tubing: Float leisurely down the river, surrounded by lush greenery.

  • Tips:
    • Book your accommodation and activities in advance, as permits are required to enter the park.
    • Engage a licensed guide for trekking and other activities.
    • Be prepared for the rainforest environment with appropriate clothing, footwear, and insect repellent.

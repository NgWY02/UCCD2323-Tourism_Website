1
Historical Melaka City Center

Immerse yourself in the rich history of Melaka's UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Dutch Square: Admire the iconic red buildings, including the Stadthuys and Christ Church.
    • A Famosa: Explore the ruins of this 16th-century Portuguese fortress.
    • St. Paul's Hill & Church: Climb the hill for panoramic views and visit the ruins of St. Paul's Church.

  • Activities:
    • Melaka River Cruise: Take a relaxing boat ride along the Melaka River, enjoying the sights and sounds of the city.
    • Jonker Street Night Market: Experience the vibrant atmosphere of this bustling night market, offering a variety of food, souvenirs, and antiques.
    • Baba & Nyonya Heritage Museum: Learn about the unique Peranakan culture and admire the beautifully preserved ancestral home.
2
Cultural & Culinary Adventures

Delve deeper into Melaka's cultural tapestry and indulge in its diverse culinary scene.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Cheng Hoon Teng Temple: Discover the oldest functioning Chinese temple in Malaysia.
    • **Kampung Kling Mosque:** Admire the unique blend of architectural styles in this historic mosque.
    • Melaka Straits Mosque: Witness the stunning sight of this mosque built on stilts over the sea.

  • Culinary Experiences:
    • Try Nyonya cuisine: Sample the distinctive flavors of Peranakan dishes at a local restaurant.
    • Explore the food stalls at Jonker Walk: Taste a variety of local delicacies and street food.
    • Visit a traditional coffee shop: Enjoy a cup of authentic Melaka coffee and local snacks.
3
Beyond the City Center

Venture beyond the city center to discover more of Melaka's attractions.

  • Options:
    • A'Famosa: Have a fun-filled day at this historical theme park with water attractions, animal shows, and more.
    • Tanjung Bidara Beach: Relax on the sandy shores and enjoy the calm waters of this beach.
    • Melaka Zoo: Encounter a variety of animals at this zoological park.

  • Tips:
    • Plan your transportation in advance, especially if you're visiting attractions outside the city center.
    • Check the opening hours and ticket prices for the attractions you plan to visit.
    • Leave some time for souvenir shopping and exploring the local markets.

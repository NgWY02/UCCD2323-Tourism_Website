Kota Bharu: Cultural Immersion

Start your Kelantan adventure in Kota Bharu, the state capital, and immerse yourself in its rich cultural heritage.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Siti Khadijah Market: Explore this vibrant market, known for its female traders and wide array of local products.
    • Istana Jahar: Discover the history of Kelantan's royal family at this beautifully preserved palace.
    • Gelanggang Seni (Cultural Centre): Experience traditional Kelantanese performances like wayang kulit (shadow puppetry) and dikir barat (choral singing).

  • Activities:
    • Visit the Handicraft Village & Craft Museum: Admire and purchase exquisite Kelantanese handicrafts.
    • Explore the Muzium Islam: Learn about the history of Islam in Kelantan.
    • Stroll through the vibrant Chinatown: Discover historical buildings and sample local Chinese cuisine.
2
Pulau Perhentian: Island Paradise

Escape to the serene beauty of the Perhentian Islands, a tropical paradise with crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and lush jungles.

  • Choose Your Island:
    • Pulau Perhentian Besar: Opt for a more relaxed and luxurious experience on this larger island.
    • Pulau Perhentian Kecil: Embrace a backpacker vibe and enjoy a lively atmosphere on this smaller island.

  • Activities:
    • Snorkeling & Diving: Discover the underwater wonders and swim alongside colorful fish and coral.
    • Beach Relaxation: Unwind on the pristine beaches, soak up the sun, and swim in the turquoise waters.
    • Jungle Trekking: Venture into the island's interior and explore its lush rainforest trails.
3
Dabong: Nature & Adventure

Experience the natural beauty and adventure of Dabong, a charming town nestled amidst lush greenery and cascading waterfalls.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Jelawang Waterfall: Trek to the majestic Jelawang Waterfall, the highest waterfall in Southeast Asia, and enjoy its breathtaking scenery.
    • Gua Ikan: Explore this unique cave filled with fish and admire its natural formations.

  • Activities:
    • Scenic Train Ride: Take a memorable train journey from Wakaf Bharu to Dabong, enjoying the picturesque views along the way.
    • Jungle Trekking: Embark on a guided trek through the rainforest and discover its hidden gems.
    • River Tubing: Experience the thrill of tubing down the clear waters of the river.
4
Pasir Puteh: Tranquility & Beaches

Conclude your Kelantan trip with a relaxing day at Pasir Puteh, known for its serene beaches and tranquil atmosphere.

  • Highlights:
    • Pantai Bisikan Bayu: Unwind on this beautiful beach, known for its whispering winds and calm waters.
    • Kampung Laut: Visit this historic fishing village and learn about its traditional way of life.

  • Activities:
    • Beach Activities: Swim, sunbathe, or take a leisurely stroll along the shore.
    • Explore the Mangroves: Take a boat tour through the mangroves and observe the diverse ecosystem.
    • Visit the Craft Museum: Admire the intricate craftsmanship of local artisans.

Kangar: The Royal Town

Begin your Perlis journey in Kangar, the state capital, and delve into its royal heritage and cultural attractions.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Kota Kayang Museum: Discover the history and traditions of Perlis' royal family at this museum housed in a former palace.
    • Masjid Alwi: Admire the elegant architecture of this historic mosque, a landmark of Kangar.
    • Perlis State Park: Explore the lush greenery and diverse flora and fauna of this protected area.

  • Activities:
    • Stroll through the Kangar Street Art: Discover vibrant murals and street art showcasing Perlis' culture.
    • Visit the Perlis Snake and Reptile Park: Encounter various snake species and other reptiles.
    • Sample local delicacies at Warung Tepi Sawah: Enjoy traditional Kelantanese cuisine in a charming setting.
2
Kuala Perlis & Gua Kelam

Head to Kuala Perlis, the gateway to Langkawi, and venture into the depths of Gua Kelam, a unique cave experience.

  • Highlights:
    • Kuala Perlis: Take a ferry to Langkawi or enjoy a seafood feast at one of the local restaurants.
    • Gua Kelam: Embark on an exciting journey through this limestone cave, once a tin mine, and marvel at its natural formations.

  • Activities:
    • Visit the Perlis Grapes Garden: Sample fresh grapes and learn about grape cultivation.
    • Explore the Kuala Perlis Bird Park: Observe a variety of bird species in this aviary.
3
Arau & Wang Kelian

Discover the royal town of Arau and experience the vibrant border town of Wang Kelian.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Masjid Negeri: Admire the grand architecture of this state mosque.
    • Wang Kelian Market: Explore this bustling border market and shop for a variety of goods from Thailand.

  • Activities:
    • Visit the Royal Gallery: Learn about the history of Perlis' royal family.
    • Hike up Bukit Ayer Recreational Forest: Enjoy nature trails and scenic views.
    • Sample local delicacies at the Wang Kelian Food Court: Try a variety of Thai and Malaysian dishes.
4
Relaxation & Nature at Tasik Melati

Conclude your Perlis trip with a peaceful day at Tasik Melati, a serene lake surrounded by lush greenery.

  • Highlights:
    • Enjoy a boat ride on the lake: Take in the scenic views and spot local wildlife.
    • Have a picnic by the lake: Relax and enjoy the tranquil atmosphere.
    • Go for a jog or cycle around the lake: Stay active and enjoy the fresh air.

  • Tips:
    • Bring your own food and drinks for a picnic.
    • Wear comfortable shoes for walking or cycling.
    • Take some time to appreciate the natural beauty of Tasik Melati.

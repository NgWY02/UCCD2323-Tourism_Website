Langkawi Island Paradise

Begin your Kedah escapade on the enchanting Langkawi Island, a duty-free haven known for its stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and vibrant marine life.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Pantai Cenang: Relax on this popular beach, lined with resorts, restaurants, and shops.
    • Langkawi SkyCab: Take a thrilling cable car ride to the peak of Gunung Mat Cincang, offering breathtaking panoramic views.
      • Ticket Prices: Adult - RM 65, Child - RM 45
    • Underwater World Langkawi: Discover the wonders of the underwater world at this fascinating aquarium.
      • Ticket Prices: Adult - RM 38, Child - RM 28

  • Activities:
    • Island Hopping: Explore the surrounding islands, including Pulau Dayang Bunting (Pregnant Maiden Island) and Pulau Beras Basah.
      • Estimated Price: Starts from RM 30 per person
    • Mangrove Tour: Venture into the mangroves and witness the diverse ecosystem.
      • Estimated Price: Starts from RM 80 per person
    • Duty-Free Shopping: Take advantage of the duty-free status and shop for chocolates, liquor, and other goods.
2
Alor Setar's Rich Heritage

Head to Alor Setar, the capital of Kedah, to immerse yourself in the state's rich history and culture.

  • Must-Visit:
    • Alor Setar Tower: Enjoy panoramic views of the city from the observation deck of this iconic tower.
      • Ticket Prices: Adult - RM20, Child - RM10
    • Zahir Mosque: Admire the architectural beauty of this historic mosque, one of the oldest and largest in Malaysia.
      • Free admission (respectful attire required)
    • Balai Besar: Explore this grand 19th-century palace, once the residence of the Kedah Sultan.
      • Ticket Prices: Adult - RM5, Child - RM2

  • Other interesting places:
    • Pekan Rabu Complex: Shop for local handicrafts and souvenirs at this bustling market.
    • Paddy Museum: Learn about the importance of rice cultivation in Kedah's history and economy.
    • Nobat Tower: See the traditional musical instruments used in royal ceremonies.
3
Paddy Fields & Gunung Keriang

Experience the tranquility of Kedah's countryside and challenge yourself with a hike up Gunung Keriang.

  • Highlights:
    • Scenic Drive through Paddy Fields: Enjoy the picturesque scenery of vast paddy fields, especially during the planting or harvesting seasons.
    • Gunung Keriang Hike: Embark on a moderate hike to the summit of Gunung Keriang for stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
    • Explore the Cave Temples: Discover the unique cave temples nestled within Gunung Keriang.

  • Tips:
    • Wear comfortable hiking shoes and bring plenty of water for the Gunung Keriang hike.
    • Respect the sanctity of the cave temples and dress modestly.
    • Capture the beauty of the paddy fields with your camera.

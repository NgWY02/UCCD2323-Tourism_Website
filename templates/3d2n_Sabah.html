Kota Kinabalu City & Islands

Start your Sabah adventure in Kota Kinabalu, the state capital. Explore the city's vibrant atmosphere and then hop on a boat to discover the stunning islands of Tunku Abdul Rahman Park.

  • Must-Visit in Kota Kinabalu:
    • Signal Hill Observatory Platform: Enjoy panoramic views of the city and the surrounding islands.
    • Atkinson Clock Tower: Admire this historic landmark, one of the oldest structures in Kota Kinabalu.
    • Sabah State Museum: Learn about Sabah's rich cultural heritage and natural history.

  • Tunku Abdul Rahman Park:
    • Island Hopping: Visit several islands in the park, such as Sapi Island, Manukan Island, and Mamutik Island.
    • Snorkeling & Diving: Discover the vibrant coral reefs and marine life.
    • Relax on the Beach: Enjoy the sun, sand, and sea on the pristine beaches.
2
Kinabalu Park & Poring Hot Springs

Embark on a scenic journey to Kinabalu Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and experience the wonders of nature.

  • Highlights:
    • Mount Kinabalu: Admire the majestic peak of Mount Kinabalu, the highest mountain in Southeast Asia.
    • Kinabalu Park: Explore the park's trails, discover diverse plant and animal life, and enjoy the breathtaking scenery.
    • Poring Hot Springs: Relax and rejuvenate in the natural hot springs surrounded by lush rainforest.

  • Tips:
    • If you're planning to climb Mount Kinabalu, book your climb well in advance and prepare for the challenging trek.
    • Wear comfortable shoes and clothing suitable for hiking.
    • Bring a camera to capture the stunning scenery.
3
Sandakan & Sepilok

Fly to Sandakan and visit the Sepilok Orangutan Rehabilitation Centre, a sanctuary for orphaned and rescued orangutans.

  • Highlights:
    • Sepilok Orangutan Rehabilitation Centre: Witness the feeding sessions and observe these gentle apes in their natural habitat.
    • Sandakan City: Explore the city's historical sites, such as the Agnes Keith House and the Sandakan Memorial Park.
    • Kinabatangan River Cruise: Take a boat trip along the Kinabatangan River to spot proboscis monkeys, crocodiles, and other wildlife. (Optional, may require additional time)

  • Tips:
    • Book your flights and accommodation in advance, especially during peak seasons.
    • Respect the orangutans and follow the rules of the rehabilitation center.
    • Consider taking a Kinabatangan River Cruise for a unique wildlife experience.

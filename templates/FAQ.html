Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to visit Malaysia is between March and October, as the weather is relatively dry and sunny.
Citizens from most countries can enter Malaysia without a visa for a stay of up to 90 days. However, it's recommended to check the visa requirements based on your nationality.
Some must-visit places in Malaysia include Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Langkawi, the Cameron Highlands, and Borneo.
Malaysia is generally a safe country for tourists. However, it's always advisable to take standard precautions to ensure your safety.
